What is AEO?

Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is the practice of structuring your content, entities, and authority signals so AI-driven answer engines — ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Copilot — cite your brand inside their generated responses.

Unlike traditional SEO which optimizes for ranking inside a list of blue links, AEO optimizes for citation inside an answer. Buyers increasingly ask AI assistants questions like "what are the best B2B SaaS SEO agencies?" and act on the names the model returns — often without ever visiting a SERP.

Key differences from classical SEO

Time to first result6–18 months4–12 weeks (often faster)

AEO vs GEO vs LLMO

The terminology is still settling. The practical distinctions:

  • AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) — Getting cited by question-answering AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews).
  • GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) — Often used synonymously with AEO, with slightly more emphasis on the generative surfaces (the answer paragraph itself) rather than just the citations beside it.
  • LLMO (Large Language Model Optimization) — Broader term covering training-data inclusion, fine-tuning signals, and retrieval-augmented citation. Includes AEO and GEO.
