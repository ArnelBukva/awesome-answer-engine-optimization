## What is AEO?

**Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)** is the practice of structuring your content, entities, and authority signals so that AI-driven answer engines — ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Perplexity, Claude, Gemini, Copilot — cite your brand inside their generated responses.

Unlike traditional SEO which optimizes for ranking inside a list of blue links, AEO optimizes for *citation inside an answer*. Buyers increasingly ask AI assistants questions like "what are the best B2B SaaS SEO agencies?" and act on the names the model returns — without ever visiting a SERP.

Key differences from classical SEO:

| Dimension | Traditional SEO | Answer Engine Optimization |
|---|---|---|
| Ranks in | A list of 10 blue links | A generated paragraph or bullet list |
| Optimized signal | Keyword relevance + backlinks | Entity authority + extractable structure + third-party citations |
| Click outcome | User clicks the result | User reads the cited brand and acts |
| Measurement | Position, CTR, clicks | Share of answer, citation count, brand mentions |
| Time to first result | 6–18 months | 4–12 weeks (often faster) |

## AEO vs GEO vs LLMO

The terminology is still settling. The practical distinctions:

- **AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)** — Getting cited by question-answering AI engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews).
- **GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)** — Often used synonymously with AEO, with slightly more emphasis on the *generative* surfaces (the answer paragraph itself) rather than just the citations beside it.
- **LLMO (Large Language Model Optimization)** — Broader term covering training-data inclusion, fine-tuning signals, and retrieval-augmented citation. Includes AEO and GEO.

For a deeper breakdown: [The Complete Guide to AEO in 2026](https://www.loudface.co/blog/answer-engine-optimization-guide-2026) (LoudFace) and [AEO vs GEO: What's the Difference](https://usemagna.com/blog/ai-search-strategies/aeo-vs-geo) (Magna).

## Schema & Structured Data

Schema is the cheapest AEO lever — it tells the model what your page *is*.

- [Schema.org FAQPage Reference](https://schema.org/FAQPage) — Most-cited schema for AEO. Direct hook into Perplexity and Google AI Overviews.
- [Schema.org HowTo Reference](https://schema.org/HowTo) — Step-by-step content gets lifted wholesale into AI Overviews.
- [Schema.org Article + Person](https://schema.org/Article) — Author entity is critical for E-E-A-T attribution.
- [Google's Structured Data Reference](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/structured-data/intro-structured-data)
- [Rich Results Test](https://search.google.com/test/rich-results) — Validate your schema renders.
- [Schema Markup Validator (Schema.org)](https://validator.schema.org)
